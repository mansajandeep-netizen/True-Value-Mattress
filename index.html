Our Story

Sleep Is Not a Luxury — It's a Foundation

True Value Mattress was born from a simple frustration: mattress stores that feel like car dealerships. Pushy sales, confusing options, and prices that make no sense.

We do things differently. Our by-appointment model gives every customer a private, relaxed experience where we take the time to understand your sleep habits, health needs, and preferences — then match you with the perfect mattress at a genuinely fair price.

Located right here in Grande Prairie, Alberta, we're your neighbours. We're not a big-box chain — we're specialists who care.
